WINNERS WEEK OF SPIRITUAL EMPHASIS

The Week of Spiritual Emphasis is a special time set aside for focused prayer, fasting, and spiritual renewal. It serves as an opportunity for believers to deepen their relationship with God, seek personal growth, and align their lives with His purpose.

“For where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them.”

At Winners Chapel CBD Johannesburg, we hold Week Of Spiritual Emphasis in the First Week of every month, Wenesday, Thursday and Friday, starting 5:30 PM. Venue: 134 Marshall Street, Johannesburg, South Africa.
