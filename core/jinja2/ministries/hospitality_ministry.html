WINNERS HOSPITALITY DEPARTMENT

The Hospitality Department is an important ministry within the church, dedicated to creating a warm, welcoming, and comfortable environment for all attendees. This department plays a significant role in ensuring that everyone—whether new visitors, regular members, or guests—feels valued and cared for from the moment they enter the church.

“ Distributing to the necessity of saints; given to hospitality.”

The Hospitality Department serves as a reflection of God’s love and care, making people feel welcome, accepted, and supported. It creates a space where everyone can experience the warmth of Christian fellowship and the love of Christ, helping to cultivate a community that is united, loving, and inclusive.
