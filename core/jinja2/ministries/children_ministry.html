WINNERS CHILDREN DEPARTMENT

The Children’s Department plays a vital role in the spiritual development and upbringing of the younger generation within the church. It is dedicated to nurturing children in their faith, providing them with the foundational teachings of the Bible in an engaging and age-appropriate manner.

“Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it.”

The Children’s Department provides opportunities for children to discover their talents and develop skills in a supportive environment. It encourages young ones to embrace their faith with joy and excitement, helping them build strong spiritual foundations that will last throughout their lives
