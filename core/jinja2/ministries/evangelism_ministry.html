WINNERS EVANGELISM DEPARTMENT

The Evangelism Department is a crucial ministry within the church, dedicated to spreading the gospel and reaching out to those who have not yet heard the message of Jesus Christ. Its primary goal is to equip believers with the tools, knowledge, and passion to share their faith and bring others into the kingdom of God.

“... Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature.”

The Evangelism Department also emphasizes the importance of prayer and support for those who are being reached, fostering a spirit of compassion and dedication in all evangelistic efforts.
