WORD OF FAITH BIBLE INSTITUTE (WOFBI)

The Word of Faith Bible Institute (WOFBI) is an essential training ground for believers who desire to deepen their understanding of the Bible and grow in their Christian faith. WOFBI offers structured courses that equip participants with solid biblical knowledge, practical ministry skills, and the tools necessary to live out their faith effectively.

“... a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.”

Graduates of WOFBI are better equipped to take on leadership roles, evangelize, and live out the gospel in every area of their lives. The institute serves as a launching pad for believers to grow in their spiritual walk, fulfill their God-given purpose, and impact the world for Christ.
