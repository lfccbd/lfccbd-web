WINNERS YOUTH ALIVE FELLOWSHIP (YAF)

The Youth Alive Fellowship (YAF) is a dynamic and vibrant ministry dedicated to empowering and equipping young people to live out their faith boldly and passionately. YAF provides a platform for youth to come together, build relationships, and engage in activities that promote spiritual growth, leadership, and service to God.

“... saith the LORD, thoughts of peace, and not of evil, to give you an expected end.”

YAF is not only about spiritual development but also focuses on social impact, leadership training, and personal growth. By providing opportunities for youth to get involved in ministry, outreach, and community service, YAF empowers the next generation to make a positive difference in their communities and the world.
